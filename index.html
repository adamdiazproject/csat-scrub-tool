✅ Valid Scrub Scenarios
The only cases where a DSAT can be scrubbed. Each must be supported with clear evidence.
1. Customer Did Not Get a Proper Chance to Interact
  • Email bounced, undelivered, or customer never saw reply and stated this in their DSAT
  • Customer claimed to have replied but no further messages received
  • Agent has no touch on ticket — only auto-reassigned under solved/pending status
  • Blank tickets: abandoned calls, SPAM, non-English where macro was sent and customer never replied
  • Tickets that only say "help" or have unclear/no issue given
  • Phone Support: agent took all steps but call did not go through
2. Customer Submitted a DSAT by Mistake
  • Customer clearly refers to another company or product unrelated to Flex
  • Customer follows up and explicitly states the rating was a mistake
3. Customer DSAT is Misaligned
  • Customer leaves negative rating but feedback is overwhelmingly positive
  • Feedback directed at company/product but customer clearly states agent did a great job
  • DSAT due to communication preference only (language, phone vs email) and agent followed SOP
  • AI-driven DSAT — scrub ONLY if evidence is clear and unambiguous
4. Exempted or Specialized Teams Only
  • Partner Care (PC), EPF, Income Verification (IV), Disputes, AI Outreach, TL Escalation
  • Known product/tool limitation and agent accurately explained or followed up
  • DSAT says "not resolved" but agent provided clear next steps and received no reply
🚫 Do Not Scrub
These must never be scrubbed, even if the agent had positive intentions.
1. DSAT Has No Comment or Blank Feedback

Without a customer explanation, we have no way of knowing what caused the negative rating. Exception: customer later clarifies it was a mistake on the same ticket.

2. Agent Contributed to the Negative Experience
  • Incorrect or misleading information provided
  • Failure to de-escalate a frustrated customer
  • Unclear, dismissive, or cold/unprofessional messaging
  • Failure to follow SOPs or escalate when appropriate
  • Issue tagged as solved prematurely
  • Any Customer Impact or AV/Regulation fails per FLEX QA Guidelines v6
⚠️ When in Doubt: Don't Scrub. Escalate to Jaylin Quiamco or Tiffany Riccardelli for borderline cases.
